Privacy Policy

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Grist Mill Capital handles information in connection with our automated trading system alert service. This is a closed, internal system used exclusively by the fund manager.

2. SMS Messaging Program

Grist Mill Capital operates an automated SMS alert system that sends trading notifications including trade executions, position exits, risk limit warnings, and system status updates. Messages are sent exclusively to the fund manager own registered mobile phone number. No messages are sent to third parties.

Opt-in: The account owner opted in by personally configuring the system with their own phone number. No public opt-in form exists.

Opt-out: Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent.

Help: Reply HELP for assistance, or contact kevin@gristmillcapital.co.

Message frequency: Varies based on trading activity. Message and data rates may apply.

3. Information We Collect

We collect only the mobile phone number provided during system configuration. No personal information is collected from third parties, and no data is sold, rented, or shared with any third party.

4. Data Security

Phone numbers and system configuration data are stored securely in local configuration files accessible only to the fund manager.
